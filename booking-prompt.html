Complete Booking Page — One Skeleton, Any Business

Build me a booking page for my business as ONE complete webpage (HTML + CSS + JavaScript in a single file). FRONTEND ONLY — no backend, no database, no login. Bookings don't need to save yet; just show a "Booking confirmed!" screen at the end. ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ MY BUSINESS ← change only this part ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ - Business name: [Your Business Name — e.g., "Sharma Clinic"] - What I do: [Your business type — e.g., "doctor's clinic"] - Brand colour: [Your brand colour — e.g., "teal green"] - I take bookings by: [TIME or DATES] - Services (name — duration — price): 1. [Service 1 — duration — price] 2. [Service 2 — duration — price] 3. [Service 3 — duration — price] - Available slots: [Your time slots — e.g., 10:00 AM, 11:30 AM, 2:00 PM, 4:00 PM, 5:30 PM] ━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━ The booking flow: 1. Show my business name + a one-line tagline at the top. 2. Step 1 — customer picks a service. 3. Step 2 — customer picks a slot (TIME slots, or a date range if DATES). 4. Step 3 — customer enters name + phone number. 5. Step 4 — clean "Booking confirmed!" screen with a summary. 6. Add a small step progress bar and a "Back" button. Design: - Mobile-first and clean — must look great on a phone. - Use my brand colour as the main accent everywhere. - Big tap-friendly buttons, rounded corners, lots of breathing room. - No clutter, no ads, no fake logos. Give me the full code in ONE file I can save as index.html and open in my browser.
