About Us

Brewing Since 2018

Chai Sutta was born from a simple belief: that the best chai comes from the best ingredients, prepared with patience. We source our tea directly from small farms in Munnar and Darjeeling.

What started as a tiny kulhad stall in Connaught Place has grown into a beloved café chain — but our philosophy hasn't changed. Every cup is still brewed to order, one at a time.
