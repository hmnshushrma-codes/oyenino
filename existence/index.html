Prompt 01
NOIR Studio ‚Äî Luxury Salon
Build me a complete landing page for a premium men's grooming salon called "NOIR Studio". Design direction: - Nature: Luxury, masculine, refined ‚Äî think high-end barbershop meets spa - Colors: Deep black background, warm gold (#c9a84c) accents, cream (#f0ece0) text. Sections: Hero with tagline, 3 service cards with prices, a testimonial, and a booking CTA. Add smooth fade-up animations and gold hover effects.
Dark theme + serif font + gold = instant luxury feel. Works for any premium service ‚Äî salon, restaurant, law firm, jewellery.
